2015-2016

In her 2nd year at Sint-Lukas, she focused further on sculpture. The concept: Global warming, herefor she sculpted a branch, scale 1m, and glazed it in silver. She placed this branch on different locations to see if it would blend in with the surroundings. The next project was again about the traditional vaseform and the scale.She is questioning the function of the vase. She got her inspiration from Blossfeldt, the German artist who photografed plants.
