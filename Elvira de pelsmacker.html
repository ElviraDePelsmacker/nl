Growing up  with parents who are artist the choice was evident to go for arts herself. When she was 12 years old she learned to know the throwing wheel. Thats when she knew thats she liked ceramics. Six year later she decided to study ceramics in Sint-Lukas school of arts in Ghent. She got to know ceramics in a knew way: the artistic way. Although she kept on practicing on throwing she did start to focus more on sculpting.  She combines both the talents of her parents in her work. Toch blijft de ambacht in de keramiek voor haar een belangrijk punt.
